Company:
	- may different tabs sila each job posts:
		1. To Review: mga applicants na di pa na viview ng company (walang action na ginagawa)
		2. Shortlisted: ito yung applicants na neither dinecline nor inofferan ng job, PERO nireview ng company and nilagay sila dito as a potential fit.
		3. Offered: dito naman yung mga applicants na sinendan ng offer to proceed or recede.
		4. Offer Accepted: mga applicants na nag accept sa offer ng company to proceed. may option na dito yung company:
			- schedule an interview: may mag popopup na modal na iaask yung company for a message (optional), date and time, and a generate google meet link button. then isesend na sa applicant. pag na sent na sa applicant, then yung button is magiging 'Interviewed' and if cinlick, mag popopup yung interview information.
			- mark as hired
			- decline (with optional reason)
		5. Offer Declined: applicants na dinecline yung offer and nag recede (with optional reasons).
		6. Rejected: mga nireject ng company at any stage
		7. Hired
		8. All: lahat ng applicants kahit anong status and sorted by the date na na update yung application nila.
